Help & FAQ

Getting Started

What is SKRR?
SKRR is a car meet community app. You create a profile card with your car info, connect with other car enthusiasts, discover and host meets, and earn Respect from the community.
How do I set up my profile?
Go to your Profile tab and tap "Edit Card." Fill in your username, car details (year, make, model, specs, mods), add photos, and choose your card style including background color and outline color.
What is my SKRR ID?
Your SKRR ID is a unique 6-character code (e.g. #JDM001) that identifies you on the platform. Others can search it to find and connect with you. You can find it displayed on your profile card.

Connecting with Others

How do I add someone?
Tap the Network tab, then ADD. You can search by SKRR ID or scan their QR code. They'll receive a connection request and need to accept before you're connected.
How do I find my QR code?
Tap ADD in the Network tab, then select the QR CODE tab. Your QR code is under MY QR. Show it to someone at a meet for them to scan and connect with you instantly.
Can I view someone's card before accepting their request?
Yes. In the Network tab under REQUESTS, tap on any incoming request to view their full FlipCard profile before deciding to accept or decline.
Can I remove a connection?
Connection management is coming in a future update.

Meets

How do I find meets?
Meets are shown on the home feed. Tap any meet card to see full details, who's going, and the location.
How do I host a meet?
Go to the Post tab and fill in your meet details — title, location, date and time, description, and car types welcome. Tap Post Meet when you're ready.
How do I join a meet?
Open a meet and tap JOIN THIS MEET. You can leave at any time before the meet ends by tapping the same button.
How do I end a meet I'm hosting?
Open your meet and tap END THIS MEET. This marks the meet as ended and opens up the Respect window so all attendees can give Respect to each other.
How do I cancel a meet?
Open your meet and tap CANCEL THIS MEET. This permanently removes the meet. This action cannot be undone.

Respect

What is Respect?
Respect is a score on your profile that reflects your reputation in the community. It's earned when other attendees give you Respect after a meet you both attended.
How do I give Respect?
After a host ends a meet you attended, a Give Respect section appears on the meet page. You can give up to 3 Respects per meet — one per person. You cannot give Respect to yourself.
Can I lose Respect?
Respect scores can only go up through normal use. We reserve the right to adjust scores if abuse is detected.
Can I give Respect to the host?
Yes. The host can also give and receive Respect just like any other attendee.

Card & Profile

How do I change my card outline color?
Go to Profile → Edit Card. Scroll to the Card Style section and pick an outline color. The color affects your card border, stats, and the SKRR logo on your card.
How do I add a car photo?
In Edit Card, tap the car photo area and select a photo from your library. Make sure to tap Save when done.
How often can I change my username?
Once every 30 days. The app will show you how many days remain until your next change is available.
What does the FlipCard show?
The front of your FlipCard shows your profile photo, username, location, car badge, and your stats (Meets, Hosted, Network, Respect). The back shows your full car specs, stats, mods, and engine details. Tap VEHICLE STATS to flip.

Account

How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Delete Account, or email support@skrr.app and we'll process it within 48 hours.
I forgot my password. What do I do?
On the login screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link shortly.

Contact

Email us at support@skrr.app — we typically respond within 24–48 hours.
